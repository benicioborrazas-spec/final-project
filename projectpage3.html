Kareem Abdul-Jabar

who is it?

Kareem Abdul-Jabbar is a former American basketball player and one of the greatest in NBA history. He played for the Milwaukee Bucks and the Los Angeles Lakers from 1969 to 1989. Known for his famous “skyhook” shot, he won six NBA championships and was a six-time MVP. Abdul-Jabbar is also known for his work as an author, actor, and activist who promotes education and social justice.
